TIC TAC TOE

About Game

Tic-Tac-Toe is certainly a game you are familiar with. You realize how easy the game is? The majority of individuals believe that. Tic-Tac-Toe isn't actually as easy as you believe, though, once you give it some serious thought. Many games, including tic-tac-toe, require players to anticipate their opponents' moves and try to predict what they could do next.

How to Play

The game is played on a three by three square grid. You are X, and your friend is O in this scenario (or the computer). Each player places a mark in an open area in turn. The winner is the first person to acquire three of her markings in a row (up, down, across, or diagonally). The game is ended when all nine squares are filled. The game finishes in a draw if no player scores three points in a succession.
